Tableau Dashboard

Sat 30 December 2017

In this article, we'll be going through the business problem and the actions performed to solve the business problem. A British company has been growing its business since its infancy stage. Their sales have been good as they deal with Office infrastructure. However, the VP Sales is not very happy with the refunds that the Company has been providing to its Customers. The VP wanted a decision driven method to be employed rather than a traditional approach to derive a strategy.

Solution: As a first step the VP planned to evaluate all the BI tools available in the market. Tableau was selected as it met his requirements. Using Tableau, he wanted his team members to give him the forecast of Sales for the next year as well as the probability of refunds category wise for a year. This will help him plan his next steps to avoid the refunds.

Actions Performed to Create the Dashboard:
1. Create Hierarchies and Folders in the dataset provided.
2. Generate a list of order returned from customers and compared it to the original sales, sort the visualization in ascending order in terms of returned order, for top 10 countries in terms of refunds.
3. Map top 10 countries, with most of the refunds on the world map.
4. Show Country Maps.
5. Predict the returned sale with lowest and actual forecast of the data.
6. Add a URL action to represent details of the countries .

Going through the above actions I generated Tableau Dashboard using multiple sheets.
